# Quote Genius - Specification

## What It Is
Instant quote generator for service businesses. No signup, no account — just fill in the details and send a professional quote link to your customer.

## How It Works
1. Business enters: name, business name, service description, line items with prices
2. App generates a shareable URL with all quote data encoded
3. Customer clicks link → sees clean, professional quote
4. Business can copy quote as text or share link

## Features

### Free Version
- Business name & contact info
- Up to 5 line items (service + price)
- Auto-calculated total
- Generate shareable quote URL
- Copy quote as formatted text

### Future (Paid)
- Custom logo upload
- Brand colors
- More than 5 line items
- Save business info for reuse
- PDF export
